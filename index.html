VRF Lite vs VRF

VRF is its full form is about Labeling (via MPLS) your traffic at layer 3 ( similar concept to Vlans at layer 2).
In VRF , traffic is isolated from source to destination through the MPLS cloud which uses MPBGP in the service provider
Cloud environment.RD and RTs (Route Distinguishers and Route Targets) are incorporated for overlapping Route segregation and using Common services.
